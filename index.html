About Me

Full Stack Web Developer with graphic design experience educated at the University of North Carolina and James Madison University. Skilled in responsive web technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as libraries such as JQuery, and Bootstrap, and architectures such as ReactJS and NodeJS. Strengths include a passion for creativity, dedication to problem-solving, and commitment to quality work. An outspoken team player with a strong desire to learn and improve his skills. Successes include improving client relationships at Cisco by developing and maintaining a customer service agent portal, increasing volunteer participation by redesigning and developing a holiday website for the Raleigh Jaycees volunteer organization, improving the user experience of a bank application for the FDIC by developing a new upgraded, responsive application, and designing a clean, organized, and intuitive finance application for medical professionals at the AAMC. Excited about the next opportunity to leverage his skills and experience to assist a company or organization succeed.

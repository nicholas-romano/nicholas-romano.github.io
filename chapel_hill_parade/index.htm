Saturday, December 9th, 10 am - 12 noon

Located in Downtown Chapel Hill & Carrboro, NC

Beginning at the old Post Office on E. Franklin Street - Ending at the Carrboro Town Hall on Main Street

Join The Excitement!

Come be a part of a wonderful holiday tradition in the Triangle... The Chapel Hill- Carrboro Holiday Parade! Experience the exciting sights and sounds of marching bands, vocalists, beautifully decorated floats, and youth group performances. The parade promises to deliver everything the holiday season has to offer including elves, reindeer, candy canes, and of course, Santa Claus!

Have a great idea for a parade float or performance you would like to showcase? Register now to save your spot in the parade.

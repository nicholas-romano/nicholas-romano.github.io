Registration

Choose one of 5 ways to register:

Community/
Non-Profit

Please register as Community or Non-Profit Groups Ticket type if your group falls into the following categories: scouts, school groups, churches, animal rescues, etc.

(Click here to register)

Business
(Walking Only)

Business walking groups only. Please select this ticket type if your business plans to walk in the Parade. If you plan to have any of the following: trucks, cars, motorcycles, bicycles, etc. in the parade, you must register as a mobile business.

(Click here to register)

Business
(Float/Mobile)

Business Sponsorship level provides entry into the parade for businesses with vehicles, floats or other mobile entry, including trucks, cars, motorcycles, bicycles, etc.

(Click here to register)

Snowflake Sponsor
(Float/Mobile)

Snowflake Sponsor - Float/Mobile provides entry into the parade for businesses with vehicles, floats or other mobile entry and provides named mention on our website, print media and by the announcer during the parade.

(Click here to register)

Star Sponsor
(Float Sponsorship)

Star Sponsor - Float Sponsorship registration will provide you with a professionally designed and driven float, with your name on the float and other sponsorship name recognition.

(Click here to register)
